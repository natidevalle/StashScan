\f1\b\fs36 \cf3 1. Overview\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\b0\fs22 \cf5 StashScan is a retrieval-first home inventory app for iOS. The core job-to-be-done is answering the question: "where did I put it?" Users label physical containers with QR codes, describe their contents, and assign them to a location hierarchy. When they need to find something, they either scan a QR code or search the app.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0\fs24 \cf0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\fs22 \cf5 The app is designed around the reality that home storage is heterogeneous \'97 containers vary in shape (boxes, bags, drawers, bins) and are nested inside zones and locations. The hierarchy is 4 levels deep: Location \uc0\u8594  Zone \u8594  Container \u8594  Items.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0\fs24 \cf0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\fs22 \cf5 Printing is handled natively via Bluetooth LE to a Phomemo Q02E thermal printer. No third-party SDK is required; the printer communicates over a documented BLE GATT profile.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb200\sa200\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0\fs24 \cf0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sb400\sa160\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f1\b\fs36 \cf3 3. User & Context\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\b0\fs22 \cf5 Single user. The primary context is a home with multiple storage areas \'97 basement shelves, Kallax units, closets, garage \'97 containing a mix of container types including cardboard boxes, plastic bins, fabric bags, and drawer organizers.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0\fs24 \cf0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\fs22 \cf5 The user's pain point is not cataloguing for its own sake, but retrieval friction: remembering which of many similar-looking containers holds a specific item, especially containers that are rarely accessed.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb120\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f0\fs24 \cf0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sb80\sa80\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0

\f2\fs22 \cf5 Usage pattern is sporadic and task-driven. The app is opened either to add a new container during an organisation session, or to find something specific during retrieval. It is not a daily-use app.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sb200\sa200\pardirnatural\partightenfactor0
